Choose Similarity Basis

Select whether to cluster countries by structural or semantic similarity.

🌲

Structural

Tree Edit Distance via Zhang-Shasha on the infobox tree. Measures how different the shape of each country's Wikipedia entry is.

O(n²m) · TED
🔤

Semantic

Jaccard coefficient over leaf text tokens — language, religion, government terminology, and cultural vocabulary.

Jaccard · Fast

Note: Structural clustering computes O(n²) TED pairs and may take several seconds for large datasets. Semantic clustering uses pre-computed Jaccard scores and is near-instant.

Choose Clustering Algorithm

Each algorithm has different characteristics. K-Medoids and Agglomerative require a target k.

HIERARCHICAL

Agglomerative

Bottom-up UPGMA hierarchical clustering. Produces a full dendrogram showing the merge order at every level. Requires k clusters.

Params: k, linkage
DENSITY

DBSCAN

Density-based clustering. Automatically detects outliers as noise (cluster −1). Good for irregular cluster shapes. No k required.

Params: eps, min_samples
GRAPH

Spectral

Graph Laplacian partitioning on the similarity matrix. Excellent at finding non-convex clusters. Requires k clusters.

Params: k
PAM

K-Medoids

PAM algorithm — cluster centre is always a real country (medoid). More robust to outliers than K-Means. Requires k clusters.

Params: k
